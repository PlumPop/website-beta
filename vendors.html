Our Story

Sounds generic doesn’t it? As though they’re just words to make us sound genuine and sugar coat underlying truths. Believe it or not, the real truth really does come down to that boring statement. Small business is a matter that’s close to heart. Our heritage of Greek and Mexican business owners stems back generations. The stories passed down to us of the obstacles they faced helped give us the motivation to want to help others just like them. Well, that’s where you come in. Helping small business’ thrive in times where many are struggling allows us to give back to not only our family, but also our community.

We hope that gives you some insight into why we’re so eager to help. And for those of you who are still skeptical of our intentions, we urge you to give it a try... or wait for your buddy to give it a try and see how he pans out.
