What is PlumPop?

You’ve got the goods. We’ve got the marketing platform. Think of us as your trusty co-pilot. We’ve created a starter digital loyalty and rewards program that focuses on independent coffee shops. We believe technology should be used to help build a better way for you to compete with the big guys. We’ve developed a program to get you on the path to keeping your current customers, attaining new ones, and having them all come back for more. You’re not alone because we’ve got your back. From beginning to end, start to finish, we’re with you.

We view our apps as an extension of your business. We take a great amount of pride and a significant amount of time when we design any experience on the platform to ensure your business is represented with your best digital foot forward.

  • We offer you complete control on what rewards you provide to your customers.
  • With fixed pricing and no revenue splitting, you keep more of the money you earn rather than divvy up your profits with a partner.
  • Customer service is a top priority for us. We will always address any inquiries or concerns in a timely manner.
  • Keep track of your account through the app on your phone to make changes or issue updates.
  • We know you don't have time to let anything slow you down. The PlumPop Rewards Station was designed to redeem your rewards or collect a token in exchange for a purchase with speed in mind. We don't want to be the ones to hold you up when you've got a line out the door.
  • Our program is flexible so you can accept any form of payment from your customers.
  • Unfortunately for those businesses that use paper punch cards, it's far too easy for someone to cheat the system. We work hard to root out fraudulent activity so your money stays where it belongs.
  • We value your customers just as much as you do. Be assured that the privacy and sensitive data of both your business and your customers is of the utmost importance to us.
  • Our system is completely contactless. Using either an Android phone or an iPhone, your customers can feel at ease when interacting with the PlumPop Rewards Station. There is no physical exchange of paper or their phone. They will also not touch any shared device.
  • Your logo is a powerful visual symbol that a customer is first introduced to on their way to loving your brand. We believe it forms a foundation for creating a loyal brand following. With just your logo, we'll take care of always putting it front and center for you.
